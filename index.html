What is reCORd?

An application for taking care of your heart.

Measurement

Our app enables you to input your weight and body water percentage levels manually or, if you prefer it that way, also import them from your iHealth cloud automatically.

Prediction

Our app's algorithm detects whether some of measurements mean that something has changed to a dangerous extent.

Notification

In case of danger or risk, our app (if you so desire) sends messages to certain people you choose (e.g. doctor, close relative, etc.).

How does it help?

By notifying certain people.

The application takes in the numbers of the people you want notified about your condition, or you can set it to only warn you. Basically, it sends SMS messages to your relatives or just sends a notification to you.

Is it complicated?

No, it's really simple.

Our interface is simple so anybody can use it. If you are not familiar with "the touchscreen", we have created the app in a way that you can still use it.
